Types of AI

ANI

ANI , as I've mentioned, stands for Artificial Narrow Intelligence . It's named that way because it can only perform tasks that were predefined and programed, but it's still a type of AI!

AGI

AGI stands for Artificial General Intelligence .
General intelligence will parallel human level intelligence.
It also implies performing general tasks and figuring out solutions without assistance .

As researchers make progress, AGI becomes more of a reality.
And as soon as that becomes a reality, we'll quickly move towards the next step, Artificial Superintelligence .

ASI

ANI is impressive and AGI can change the way we live, but both can't compare to the future of AI, Artificial Superintelligence.
