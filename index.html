What is AI ?

First of all. AI stands for Artificial intelligence, and is just a fancy way to say Intelligence created by humans .

To understand AI we need to understand that when we talk about intelligence we talk about the abillity of gathering , processing and then applying knowledge, respectively.

Truth is...

... there are different types of AI, all varying in their level of intelligence!
If we take a closer look, we can see that AI is all around us, just not as intelligent as science-fiction stories portray it to be.

Different types you said?

YES. As I 've mentioned, there are different types of AI.
The most basic form of AI is ANI , or Artificial Narrow Intelligence - it's called Narrow because it can only perform tasks in specific, predifined context .
Narrow AI can do a task it was programmed to incredibly well, but... and here's the catch... when you present a task that wasn't predefined and programmed, the AI get's confused, hence the name Narrow .
