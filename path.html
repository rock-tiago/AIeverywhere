What Else?

AIs are more useful when we can communicate with them easily. For that they need NLP , or Natural language processing. With natural language processing, researchers make machines understand things just like a human would. But that's harder than it sounds, as machines don't understand irony, for instance.
Speech recognition is excellent for getting input, but machines also need to learn form gathered data. For that there's a whole domain of AI called machine learning.

Machine learning

Machine learning is such a vital step that is often confused for AI.
The simple version of machine learning is this: we give a machine a lot of data regarding a topic. Let's say, images of cats.

After showing it hundreds or even thousands of cat pictures, we then give it a test. For example, asking the name of the animal in the picture.
